Learn how to play Tank in OW2

By Nbow

Profiles

X

Doomfist

Type: Dive.

Play Style: In and out play style to force cool downs, upset positioning and rotation and find cheap kills.

Good Maps: Maps with good high ground.

Counters: Winston & Ramattra.

Countered By: D.Va, Junker Queen & Orisa.

X

D.Va

Type: Dive.

Play Style: Finding off angles to apply pressure from and counter enemy off angles.

Good Maps: Maps with good high ground.

Counters: Winston, Wrecking Ball, Roadhog, Doomfist, Reinhardt & Orisa.

Countered By: Zarya.

X

Junker Queen

Type: Rush

Play Style: Fast Rush on frontline.

Good Maps: Maps with little high ground and lots of congested areas.

Counters: Winston, Wrecking Ball & Doomfist.

Countered By: Orisa, Reinhardt, Roadhog, Sigma & Zarya.

X

Orisa

Type: Rush/Poke

Play Style: Slow/Fast Rush, can play Poke or can Rush backline.

Good Maps: Maps with little high ground.

Counters: Doomfist, Roadhog, Reinhardt & Junker Queen.

Countered By: D.Va, Sigma, Winston, Wrecking Ball & Zarya.

X

Ramattra

Type: Rush/Poke

Play Style: Slow/Fast Rush, can play Poke or can Rush.

Good Maps: Maps with little high ground and lots of congested areas.

Counters: Reinhardt, Roadhog & Sigma.

Countered By: Wrecking Ball & Doomfist.

X

Reinhardt

Type: Rush

Play Style: Fast Rush, holding corners & clearing high grounds.

Good Maps: Maps with little high ground and lots of congested areas.

Counters: Junker Queen, Sigma & Zarya.

Countered By: D.Va, Orisa, Ramattra, Roadhog, Winston & Wrecking Ball.

X

Roadhog

Type: Rush/Poke

Play Style: Slow/Fast Rush, can play Poke or can Rush.

Good Maps: Maps with little high ground and open areas.

Counters: Wrecking Ball, Winston, Junker Queen, Doomfist, Reinhardt & Ramattra.

Countered By: D.Va, Orisa, Ramattra, Sigma & Zarya.

X

Sigma

Type: Poke

Play Style: Slow Poke, play from distance and try to hold angles.

Good Maps: Maps with longs of range, high ground and angles.

Counters: Roadhog, Zarya, Junker Queen & Orisa.

Countered By: Ramattra, Reinhardt and Winston.

X

Winston

Type: Dive

Play Style: Can play Rush Dive with a Lucio to hard engage back line or can play normal Dive with Ana or build nanos and gain space with soft engages.

Good Maps: Maps with lots of high ground and cover.

Counters: Zarya, Sigma, Orisa & Reinhardt.

Countered By: D.Va, Doomfist, Junker Queen & Roadhog.

X

Wrecking Ball

Type: Dive

Play Style: Coordinate dives on back line from angles and small engages to force cool downs.

Good Maps: Maps with lots of open space & angles.

Counters: Ramattra, Reinhardt, Zarya & Orisa.

Countered By: D.Va, Junker Queen & Roadhog.

X

Zarya

Type: Rush

Play Style: Rush on frontline, can play a slow pace.

Good Maps: Maps with short range & congested areas.

Counters: D.va, Orisa, Roadhog, Junker Queen & Doomfist.

Countered By: Reinhardt, Sigma, Winston & Wrecking Ball.

X

Poke

Play Style: Slow fights, long distance & many angles.

Good Maps: Long range, open space & many angles.

Typical Comps: Poke (Sigma, Hanzo/Widow/Sojourn/Ashe, Bap & Zen).

X

Dive

Play Style: Can play slow dive for soft engages to build ults and then make hard engages with normal dive or can play rush dive to make hard engages on back line.

Good Maps: High ground and cover.

Typical Comps: Dive (Winston/D.Va, Tracer, Echo/Sombra/Ashe/Genji, Ana, Brig/Kiriko) or Rush Dive (Winston, Tracer, Reaper/Sombra, Moira & Lucio).

X

Rush

Play Style: Fast fights, short distance & few angles.

Good Maps: Short range, closed space & few angles.

Typical Comps: Rush (Reinhardt/Ramattra/Zarya, Reaper/Mei/Genji/Cassidy/Symettra, Baptiste & Lucio).
